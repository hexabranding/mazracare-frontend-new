Mazra Care Policy Guidelines

Safety Policy

  • All Mazra Care staff follow strict safety protocols during installation and maintenance.
  • Use of PPE is mandatory on all sites.

Environmental Policy

  • Commitment to zero-soil farming methods.
  • Maximum water recycling.
  • Organic and biodegradable media and pest control.

Farm Maintenance Guidelines

  • Daily monitoring of pH, EC, and water levels is required.
  • Pruning and cleaning schedules must be followed.
  • Monthly checkups and calibration of systems.

Crop Rotation & Management

  • Suggested crop rotation plans are provided to maintain soil-free nutrient balance.
  • Seasonal adaptation strategies included in training.

Harvesting & Post-Harvest

  • Proper handling procedures during harvest.
  • Post-harvest cold storage and packaging support available.

Sales & Distribution Policy

  • Option to partner with Mazra Care for market distribution.
  • Profit-sharing terms or fixed-price purchase models available.
