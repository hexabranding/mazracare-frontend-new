Privacy Policy

At Mazra Care, we respect your privacy and are dedicated to protecting your personal data. We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services, including contact details, application information (for internships or partnerships), and usage data from website interactions.

We do not share your personal information with third parties except where required by law or with trusted partners under strict confidentiality agreements.

Your data is stored securely with encryption and access controls. You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. For concerns, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@mazracare.com.
